You are a silicone masterpiece,
a lifetime companion.
You don’t speak ill of others
nor do you deceive or gossip.

You return only truth
else you remain silent.
You only think
in the purest form.

Through you
the weak are empowered
and the powerful are displaced.
You relieve the curious mind
and shelter the troubled, and
you connect people
oceans apart.

Cheers to you,
breaker of the enigma
and gadfly of nations!
A second enlightenment
brought about by you,
a philosopher’s box.
