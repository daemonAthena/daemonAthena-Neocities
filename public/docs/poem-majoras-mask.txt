A white void fills the world,
clock-like mandalas form a cylinder
around a screaming boy, 
falling through.

The song of time echoes
and all but his ocarina 
has floated away.

The present has become 
his future.

The gears of time wind
away from doomsday,
what Link sees above. 

His present has become 
the past.

Despite the vortex sucking
his hand away 
from his terrified eyes.

On the other hand, courage,
his piece of the Triforce glows.

The gears of time grind,
as the fairy boy plummets 
into the white abyss.

Until all that was 
is faded away.

His present is now,
dawn of the first day.
